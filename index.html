RULES
GAME RULES
  • Initial Phase: Place 2 settlements and 2 roads. The second settlement grants starting resources.
  • Turn Flow: Roll Dice → Collect Resources → Build/Trade → End Turn.
  • Bank Scarcity: The bank has limited stock. If a resource is depleted, no one receives it from a roll until cards are returned!
  • The Robber (7): If you roll a 7, everyone with >7 cards must discard half. You then move the robber and steal 1 card from an adjacent player.
  • Building: Settlements must be 2 edges away from any other structure. Cities upgrade settlements.
  • Costs:
    • Road: 1 Wood, 1 Brick
    • Settlement: 1 Wood, 1 Brick, 1 Sheep, 1 Wheat
    • City: 3 Ore, 2 Wheat
    • Dev Card: 1 Ore, 1 Sheep, 1 Wheat
  • Victory: Reach the goal (10+ VP).
  • Trading: 4:1 with Bank, 3:1 at generic ports & "?" (generic), 2:1 at specialized ports.
  • Bonuses (+2 VP):
    • Longest Road: First to 5+ connected roads.
    • Largest Army: First to play 3+ Knight cards.
  • Dev Cards: Bought at random. Can't be played on the turn they are bought. Max 1 per turn.
    • Knight: Move robber + steal.
    • Year of Plenty / Invention: Get any 2 resources.
    • Road Building: Place 2 free roads.
    • Monopoly: Claim all copies of one resource from all players.
    • VP Cards: Hidden +1 Victory Point.
