QUICK RULES
  • Initial Phase: Click a vertex for settlement, then an adjacent edge for a road. Repeats twice.
  • Turn Flow: Roll Dice → Distribute Resources → Build → End Turn.
  • Building: Settlement must be 2 edges away from any other structure.
  • Costs:
    • Road: 1 Wood, 1 Brick
    • Settlement: 1 Wood, 1 Brick, 1 Sheep, 1 Wheat
    • City: 3 Ore, 2 Wheat (Upgrades Settlement)
  • Victory: Reach 10 points (Settlement=1, City=2).
